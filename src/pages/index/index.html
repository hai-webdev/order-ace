BUSINESS ACQUISITION Ltd

Ace Global Business Acquisition Limited is a blank check company incorporated in the British Virgin Islands as a business company with limited liability for the purpose of effecting into a merger, share exchange, asset acquisition, share purchase, recapitalization, reorganization or similar business combination with one or more businesses or entities.

Our objectives are to generate compelling attractive returns for our shareholders and to enhance value through top line growth and hands on operational improvement for our potential target companies.

Our efforts to identify and complete a business combination will not be limited to any particular industry or geographic region, although our primary areas of focus will be gaming and e-commerce sector in the Greater China, Japan and Southeast Asia regions. We intend to source, evaluate, initiate and complete the business acquisition with the potential high growth businesses, and the details will be elaborated under “Acquisition Strategy”.

Consistent with our core investment principles and business strategy, we will focus our sourcing on high-quality companies that demonstrate the characteristics set out under “Investment Criteria”. We will use these criteria and guidelines in target businesses examination, but we may decide to complete our business combination with a target business that does not meet all of these criteria.
